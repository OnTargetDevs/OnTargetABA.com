Insurance FAQ

Quick answers about coverage.

We accept most insurances. Generally, insurance companies are required by law to cover ABA therapy for children with autism diagnoses, however there are some exceptions. If you are not sure if your insurance covers ABA therapy give us a call and one of our insurance experts can verify if you are covered.

Yes. We accept Medicaid plans.

If your plan isn't listed, don't worry. We routinely work with families whose carriers aren't in our standard network. Contact us and we'll verify your benefits, explore single-case agreements, and walk you through any out-of-pocket considerations before treatment begins.

You'll typically need (1) an autism diagnosis report, (2) a doctor's recommendation for ABA therapy if your insurance requires one, and (3) your insurance information so we can verify coverage. Most insurances are required to cover ABA therapy — we'll help you check.

We move quickly. After we collect your information, we coordinate and communicate with your insurance company on your behalf to obtain approval for requested services. Most families can start ABA therapy as soon as your insurance sends us their approval.
