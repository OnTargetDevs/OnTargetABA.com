About the Mayfield Village clinic

Cleveland's expanded ABA therapy center.

Our Mayfield Village center on Beta Drive has just finished a major build-out, doubling our therapy space and adding new sensory rooms, gross-motor zones, and dedicated family meeting spaces — all built specifically for kids on the spectrum and the families who love them. We've been serving Greater Cleveland for years, and the expansion lets us welcome more families with no waitlist.
