Therapy at home

Skills practiced where they actually matter.

Our team will provide your child with ABA therapy sessions, multiple times per week, in the comfort and safety of your own home. We get to observe your child in real life and help them develop practical skills that can be incorporated in everyday living.

Everything, from the initial assessment by the BCBA to everyday direct ABA therapy and family training can take place within your own home.

Advantages of In-Home ABA Therapy
  • ✓Convenience of not having to commute to a clinic.
  • ✓Familiarity of your child's own home
  • ✓Hands-on support to help teach self-help skills in the real-life environment.
  • ✓More opportunities for parent participation in sessions.
  • ✓Our clinicians can regularly observe your child in their natural setting.
  • ✓Easily allows for generalization of skills learned in ABA to everyday life.
What a session looks like

A day, woven into yours.

Naturalistic, individualized, and built around your family's rhythm — every routine becomes a learning moment.

Real-life skills

Communication, behavior management, self-care routines (dressing for the day, brushing teeth), and household tasks — all in the spaces they happen.

Social & emotional regulation

Social skills and emotional regulation are targeted within the natural context of family interactions and daily activities.

Data & progress

Therapists create individualized treatment plans, collect and analyze data during sessions, and regularly review progress — adjusting as your child grows.

Parents at the center

Family involvement is a cornerstone.

Family involvement is a cornerstone of In-Home ABA Therapy, with parents and caregivers participating in sessions to learn strategies for reinforcing skills. Therapists provide ongoing coaching and support, ensuring families feel confident in applying techniques during daily routines and interactions.
