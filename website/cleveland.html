FAQ · Cleveland

Questions Cleveland parents actually ask.

Short, honest answers. If yours isn’t here, call us — we’ll take the time to talk it through.

Where is your Cleveland clinic actually located?

Our clinic serves Cleveland from 767 Beta Dr, Suite C, Mayfield Village, OH 44143 — right off I-271, about 20 minutes east of downtown Cleveland. Most eastside suburbs are inside a 10-minute drive. See the Mayfield Village location page for parking, photos, and a map.

Which Cleveland-area suburbs do you serve?

For center-based ABA we welcome families from across Greater Cleveland — including Beachwood, Cleveland Heights, Pepper Pike, Lyndhurst, Highland Heights, South Euclid, Mayfield Heights, Gates Mills, Solon, Shaker Heights, and University Heights. In-home ABA generally covers the same eastside footprint; call us with your zip code and we’ll confirm.

Do you take our insurance?

Likely yes. We’re in-network with Ohio Medicaid and the major commercial carriers serving Cleveland-area families. For most families the out-of-pocket cost is $0. The fastest way to know for sure is to call (216) 343-1198 — we’ll verify benefits live on the phone.

My child doesn’t have an autism diagnosis yet. Can you still help?

Call us anyway. We can talk through what you’re seeing and point you to next steps. Most insurance plans require a formal diagnosis before ABA can begin — if that’s the holdup, ask us about our autism testing pathway and we’ll help you skip the long Cleveland-area waitlists.

How quickly can we start?

Most Cleveland-area families go from first phone call to first session in under 72 hours. The intake call takes about 15 minutes. We do the insurance legwork, schedule an assessment with a BCBA, and slot you into the next opening — usually that same week.
