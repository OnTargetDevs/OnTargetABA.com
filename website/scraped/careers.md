## Hero / H1

**Careers**

Notable feature: Salt Lake City, UT location now open.

## Join Us!

"Are you fun and energetic? Good with kids? Capable of multitasking? Want to change the lives of children with autism? You may be a great fit for our team! We're always looking to hire new BCBAs and RBTs to join our team. Good therapists are key to the success of our clients and we take care of our staff. We love feedback and are always looking for ways to improve our employee experience."

## Make a Difference

"The work of an ABA therapist is extremely fulfilling."

"Imagine working with a child who at first has almost no communication skills, and watching their constant progress and evolvement from your work. Imagine watching a child who seems to have no connection to their outside world learn how to consistently make eye contact, progress to pointing for things, advance to saying words, and eventually be able to communicate fully."

## We're Here for You

"Your assigned BCBA will give you clear training and modeling on all aspects of the treatment plan, making sure you're well adjusted and know exactly what to do. They will always be available for your questions and feedback. You'll learn how to do all of this in the course of natural activities and play as you have a great time with the children!"

## Work Benefits

- Competitive pay
- Pay for travel time between clients
- Room for growth
- Flexible hours

## We offer the best supervision package you can find anywhere:

"FREE BCBA Supervision. Our supervisors are professional and reliable, and you can rest assured that you'll get sufficient BCBA supervision hours. We'll train you in on everything you need to know about being a BCBA before you even sit for your exam (different assessments, treatment plans, using the 'back end' of ABA programming software, how to train in therapists, conducting parent meetings, and much more)! We offer our supervisees paid work that qualifies for the 'unrestricted hours' portion of the BACB supervision requirements, in addition to direct work with clients."

Mentorship callout: "Need Supervision Hours? LEARN ABOUT OUR MENTORSHIP PROGRAM" → https://ontargetaba.com/careers/#weOffer
