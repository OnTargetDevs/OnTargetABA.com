## Core Outcomes Section

### Better Language & Communication
Through the use of natural settings and utilizing "teachable moments" to strengthen effective language and communication skills.

### Better Play & Social Skills
Through 1-on-1 and group sessions using creative play, functional play, independent play, and social skills.

### Better Daily Living Skills
Through real-life practice and instruction with increased frequency and intensity.

## Why Choose On Target ABA (6 Benefits)

### No Waitlist / Start Services ASAP
Get started in less than 72 hours. Our fast intake process can get your child started with ABA therapy quicker than you think.

### Flexible Scheduling
We open early and stay late to suit your schedule. Not ours.

### Family Focused
We're center-based but family-focused. We're understanding and accommodating to family situations, dynamics schedules. We wouldn't have it any other way.

### Low BCBA to Client Ratio
To properly monitor your child's progress to ensure maximum progress in the shortest time.

### Exceptional Results
Our proven track record of helping children with autism both socially and functionally is something we're proud of and would like to offer you.

We provide personalized, compassionate ABA therapy services to the families and children we serve.
