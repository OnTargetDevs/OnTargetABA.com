Cookie & Privacy Preferences

A short, plain-English explanation of the cookies we use — and how to control them.

Manage preferences Read full privacy policy

A cookie is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored on your device by your browser. We use cookies to keep the site working, understand how visitors use it, and (with your consent) to personalize what you see. Detailed disclosures are managed by our cookie tool — click Manage preferences above to view and change your settings at any time.

Strictly necessary

Required for the site to function — things like remembering your session and ensuring secure browsing. These can't be switched off.

Performance & analytics

Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. We use Google Analytics. You can opt out at any time.

Functional

Remember choices like language, location, or accessibility preferences to make your next visit smoother.

Targeting & advertising

Used (sparingly) to measure ad performance and help families discover our services. You can opt out from these at any time.

Change your mind

Manage your preferences any time.

You can review or update what you've agreed to whenever you'd like. Your choices are saved on your device — clearing cookies will reset them.
