What to Expect From Local, Center-Based ABA Therapy

Our ABA centers provide a warm, safe, and encouraging environment where children learn through play, exploration, and individualized support. Instead of sitting at a desk doing repetitive tasks, your child will engage in fun, meaningful activities that build confidence and promote learning across all areas of development.

Center-based ABA therapy offers a community setting that encourages age-appropriate learning and social interaction. We see amazing progress in communication, peer relationships, academics, self-regulation, potty training, and many other essential life skills — all through personalized programs designed for each child's unique needs.

Advantages of Center-Based ABA Therapy
  • ✓Customized ABA environment improves therapy sessions
  • ✓Constant session oversight by our Autism Clinical Leadership Team
  • ✓Gross motor and sensory rooms designed for young children with autism
  • ✓Group times to work on social skills
  • ✓Preschool readiness program, including circle times
  • ✓Themed days your child will love

Our center-based ABA therapy ensures your child receives the focused care they need in a nurturing environment, right in your community.

A day at the center

Three skill areas. One full day of learning.

Every session is mapped to evidence-based goals across language, play, and school readiness — and adjusted as your child grows.

01

Language, Communication & Social Skills

  • · Making eye contact
  • · Tacting (labeling items)
  • · Manding (expressing requests and needs)
  • · Imitation
  • · Listener responding skills
  • · Responding to greetings & farewells
  • · Initiating farewells and greetings
  • · Communicative exchanges with peers
  • · Joint attention
  • · Intraverbal skills
  • · Higher-level intraverbal skills (carrying on conversations)
02

Play Skills

  • · Creative play (incorporating imagination into play)
  • · Functional play (playing with items in their intended way)
  • · Independent play (ability to play with toys by themselves)
  • · Social play (playing with peers)
Learning happens best when it feels like fun.
03

School Readiness & Academic Goals

  • · Transitioning smoothly (between activities, situations and locations)
  • · Task completion
  • · Recognizing numbers and letters
  • · Matching
  • · Counting
  • · Reading
  • · Vocabulary
  • · Math skills
Flexible Scheduling

We work with your schedule.

We know you have a busy schedule but also want your child to receive the help they need. That's why our centers open at 8 am and close at 6 pm. We work with each parent to develop a set therapy schedule that's both convenient for them and promotes the success of their child.

Need to drop off your child at 8:00 AM? No problem. Need your child to stay till 5:30 PM? No problem. We're here for you. You shouldn't have to worry about scheduling your child's therapy. You have enough to worry about. We'll work with your schedule. Because receiving ABA therapy services should be easy and convenient. We'd want it no other way.
